Goshen Christian Montessori School

GCMS is a Registered Childcare Ministry operated by Epic Ministries.
Our goal is to create an environment that allows students to gain independence, confidence, emotional and social stability, and a greater appreciation of the world around them.

Classes

Toddler Class, ages 16 months-3 years. Primary Class ages 3-6 years. We are located inside Assembly Mennonite Church at 727 New York St., Goshen, IN. Our school meets during the school year, M-F, with options to attend weekly for 3, 4, or 5 days. AM Class: 8:45-11:30 Full Day Class: 8:45-2:30 Early and After Care are available as needed.
